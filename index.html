Watershed Dashboard Builder

Create interactive watershed dashboards for external partners

Getting Started

Upload your GeoJSON files to build a customized watershed dashboard. Streams and Municipalities will be automatically fetched from PA DEP and clipped to your watershed boundary. The exported dashboard will be a self-contained package that can be shared with external partners.

Produced by the Penn State Agriculture and Environment Center | Jackson Bouffard, GIS Technician

Required Layers

Project Points Point layer (.geojson) or ZIP bundle with photos
Watershed Boundary Polygon layer with watershed extent
Stream Layer Auto-fetched from PA DEP and clipped to watershed
Will be fetched after watershed is uploaded
Municipality Boundaries Auto-fetched from PennDOT and clipped to watershed
Will be fetched after watershed is uploaded
Parcel Data (Optional) Paste county parcel REST service links (up to 3)
Enter at least one parcel service URL and upload watershed first
